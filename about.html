🏪 About Umiya Wholesales

Your trusted partner for premium quality grocery and household items in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Serving families and businesses since 2005.

Our Story

Umiya Wholesales and Retail Hub was founded with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality groceries and household items at fair and transparent prices. What started as a small neighborhood store has grown into a trusted wholesale and retail hub serving thousands of families across Ahmedabad.

We believe that every family deserves access to fresh, high-quality food at affordable prices. That's why we source directly from trusted farmers and manufacturers, cutting out unnecessary middlemen to pass the savings on to you.
